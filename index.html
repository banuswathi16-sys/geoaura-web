About Us

At GeoAura Spatial Consulting, we turn complex geographic data into clear, actionable insights that drive smarter decisions. Our end-to-end geospatial services empower businesses, governments, and institutions to visualize, analyze, and understand spatial patterns that shape the world around them.

Our Services

GIS Services
Accurate and reliable GIS solutions including spatial analysis, database creation, and customized mapping for planning and decision-making.
Toposheet & Old Map Digitization
We digitize toposheets and legacy maps into fully editable, georeferenced GIS formats with clean topology and precise attributes.
Land Use & Land Cover Map
High‑accuracy LULC mapping using satellite and aerial data to support urban planning, environmental studies, and resource management.
Land Parcel & Cadastral Mapping
Precise land parcel mapping for cadastral, revenue, and planning purposes, including boundary delineation and GIS-ready databases.
Drone Image Processing
Processing high‑resolution drone imagery to generate orthomosaics, DEMs, DSMs, contours, and 3D models with survey‑grade accuracy.
AutoCAD Services
Professional AutoCAD services including precise 2D drafting, detailed floor plans, elevations, sections, and high‑quality 3D modeling.
